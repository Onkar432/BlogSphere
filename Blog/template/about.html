Welcome to the Blog App

This blog app allows you to create, edit, and delete blog posts, as well as view posts created by other users.

Creating a New Post

To create a new post, simply click on the "New Post" button on the homepage. You will be redirected to a form where you can enter the title and content of your post.

Editing a Post

To edit an existing post, click on the "Edit" button next to the post you wish to edit. You will be redirected to a form where you can make changes to the post's title and content.

Deleting a Post

To delete a post, click on the "Delete" button next to the post you wish to delete. You will be prompted to confirm the deletion before the post is permanently removed from the database.

Viewing Posts

To view all posts, click on the "All Posts" button on the homepage. This will display a list of all posts created by all users.

Viewing User-Specific Posts

To view posts created by a specific user, click on the user's name in the navigation bar. This will display a list of all posts created by that user.
